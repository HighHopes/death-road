Enter the world

Bound by chains, but now I am free

You travel forward through the ominous portal revealed only during a full moon. You're immediately met by a disturbing world. The air is thick with smoke, obstructing your vision beyond a few meters. The light plays tricks on your eyes as shadows from a new landscape make your imagination go wild.
In spite of the treacherous landscape you're at ease and you feel unsecure in this world. This world is certainly unwelcoming, but with so much to discover you can't help but explore...

The world...

All around you you smell traces of creatures nobody could've ever imagined. While they appear to be docile and safe, you keep your distance and an eye on them. You see traces of gliding creatures, bulky creatures, and what you think might be sliding creatures of some sort.


With a final check of your supplies you ready yourself as you begin your life as a wanderer, explorer, and adventurer in this new world...
